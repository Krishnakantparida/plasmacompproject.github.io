About P464 Term paper

Term paper project is a part of academic assessment required as evaluation for Plasma Physics and Magnetohydrodynamics (P464) course. The term paper is a Computational Project meant for studying certain aspects of the galactic dynamo problem, which is concerned with explaining the origin and nature of magnetic fields residing in the interstellar gas of a galaxy.

Progression of the Term Paper Project

The Project is divided into three sections, with each of the sections representing the approach taken to progress towards the finl objective of the project.Those are listed as follows

  • [Objective-1]: Solving magnetic diffusion equation
  • [Objective-2]: Solving mean-field α-Ω dynamo equation
  • [Objective-3]: Testing equations for critical dynamo number
